Welcome to FeelingBetterNow®

FeelingBetterNow is a private and anonymous online mental health platform available to you and your family (18 years and over)

Using FeelingBetterNow® is easy!
Complete Your Assessment - Instantly understand your risk across thirteen mental health disorders by completing our gold-standard online assessment.
Review Your Results - FeelingBetterNow® immediately generates results that inform you of your diagnostic risk for a mental health disorder.
Take Action - Use the self-care plans and mental health resources available from FeelingBetterNow® and your organization. Share your results and plans with your health-care provider.
Track your progress - You can complete the assessment as often as needed. We recommend that you reassess and track your progress every 4-6 weeks.
